Agent Assignment Brief

Welcome to your first operational assignment with the Lugus Centre for Technology Safety. This document outlines your mission objectives, cover identity, and the specific tasks you are expected to carry out during this high-stakes operation.

Mission Overview

Your mission is to infiltrate Raktus Island under the guise of working for a proxy company, Genovia Supply Co., which provides essential office supplies and stationery to government facilities on the island. This cover will grant you special access to various locations, including the highly restricted areas where Project Horus is being developed.

Cover Identity

Your cover identity is as an employee of Genovia Supply Co., specifically as a logistics coordinator responsible for overseeing the delivery and management of office supplies. This role will allow you to move freely within the government buildings and research facilities on Raktus Island without raising suspicion.

Objectives

1. Identify Key Personnel: Use your access to identify and monitor the key individuals involved in Project Horus. Pay particular attention to those with direct access to the AI’s core development.

2. Gather Intelligence: Leverage your role to gather as much information as possible about the AI's capabilities, training methods, and intended applications. Your observations should focus on the AI’s interactions with human operators and any signs of autonomous decision-making.

3. Document Unusual Activity: Report any abnormal behavior or developments related to the AI that could indicate a risk of it surpassing its intended control parameters. This includes observing any interactions between the AI and government officials.

4. Secure Access Points: Identify any security weaknesses in the facility’s infrastructure that could be exploited in the event of an emergency extraction or further infiltration. This includes monitoring communication channels and entry/exit points.

Operational Details

Deployment Date: You will be deployed to Raktus Island in three days. Your first task will be to familiarize yourself with the island’s layout and the locations where Genovia Supply Co. operates. Ensure that you maintain your cover at all times, especially when interacting with government personnel.

Duration: The initial phase of the operation is expected to last six months, during which you will provide regular updates through secure communication channels. Any immediate threats or discoveries that require urgent attention should be reported immediately.

Security Protocols

Your safety and the integrity of this operation are of the utmost importance. As such, adhere strictly to the following security protocols:

1. Maintain Cover: Always present yourself as a dedicated logistics coordinator. Do not deviate from your cover identity under any circumstances.

2. Secure Communication: Use only the secure channels provided by the Lugus Centre for transmitting sensitive information. Never discuss mission details over unsecured lines.

3. Emergency Procedures: In the event of a compromised cover or imminent danger, you will be provided with an emergency extraction plan. Familiarize yourself with the protocol upon arrival.

Conclusion

This operation is critical to preventing the potential misuse of Project Horus, which poses a significant threat to global security. Your role is vital to gathering the intelligence needed to assess and neutralize this threat. Stay focused, vigilant, and above all, maintain your cover at all times.

We are counting on your success. Good luck, Agent .
