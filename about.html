The Lugus Institute

The Lugus Institute is a globally recognized think tank dedicated to safeguarding humanity from the potential risks posed by emerging technologies, particularly artificial intelligence. Our mission is to ensure that technological advancements contribute positively to society and do not compromise human autonomy, safety, or ethics.

The Lugus Institute is headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, a hub for international cooperation and home to numerous global organizations. Our location enables us to engage with a wide network of partners and experts from around the world, ensuring that our work is informed by diverse perspectives and cutting-edge research.

In addition to our Geneva headquarters, we have satellite offices in key locations such as Silicon Valley, London, and Tokyo, allowing us to maintain close ties with technology leaders and innovators across the globe.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of visionary technologists and ethicists, the Lugus Institute was established in response to the rapid acceleration of AI research and development. Named after the ancient god Lugus, a symbol of protection and wisdom, the Centre embodies the principle of using knowledge and foresight to guard against potential dangers.

Over the years, the Lugus Centre has grown from a small research group into an influential organization with a global reach. We have partnered with governments, academic institutions, and industry leaders to promote responsible AI development and to create frameworks for mitigating the risks associated with superintelligent AI.

What We Do

At the Lugus Centre, we conduct cutting-edge research into the safety, ethics, and societal impact of advanced technologies. Our work spans a wide range of activities, including:

Research: We analyze potential risks associated with AI and other emerging technologies, publishing our findings to inform public policy and industry practices.
Advocacy: We engage with policymakers and global organizations to promote regulations and standards that prioritize human safety and ethical considerations in technology development.
Education: We offer training programs, workshops, and seminars to raise awareness about the responsible use of technology among industry professionals and the general public.
Incident Response: We have a specialized team dedicated to monitoring and responding to technological threats in real-time, helping to prevent and mitigate incidents that could pose a danger to society.
