Letter of deployment for Agent

Welcome to the Lugus Institute, where only the best and brightest are chosen to protect humanity from the perils of unchecked technological advancement. Your recruitment into the Centre was no small feat — among thousands of applicants, you stood out not only for your exceptional skills and intellect but also for your unwavering dedication to the principles of safety, ethics, and human well-being.

As a new agent at Lugus, you will be working under the direct supervision of one of our most experienced operatives, Agent James Hawkins. Agent Hawkins has been instrumental in numerous high-stakes operations and is renowned for his strategic acumen and expertise in handling advanced technological threats.

Your first assignment is of utmost importance and carries significant risk. We have recently uncovered information about a secret project involving the development of superintelligent AI. The existence of this project was brought to our attention by a whistleblower, who courageously provided us with critical data that sheds light on the scope and intent behind this AI initiative. After rigorous analysis, we have determined that the risk is severe enough to warrant an undercover operation — only the second such operation in our history.

For the next six months, you will be deployed to gather more intelligence on this project. Your mission is to infiltrate the organizations behind this AI development, identify key players, and collect evidence that will allow us to dismantle this threat from the inside. The information we have gathered so far paints a grim picture, but with your help, we can prevent a catastrophe that could reshape the world as we know it.

This will not be an easy task, but we are confident in your abilities. Remember, at the Lugus Institute, we operate with the highest levels of secrecy and integrity. Your actions over the next six months will be critical to our success, and we trust you to carry out your duties with the utmost professionalism and discretion.

Welcome to the front lines, Agent. The future of humanity may very well rest in your hands.
